About Annika


My name is Annika Chavez. I love to create ideas and conceptualize as well as play a part in the implementation and development. I have always been interested in digital art and graphic design as a kid. In high school, I began to teach myself programming in order to further utilize my skills. My current abilities range solely from art and design, to implementing the art into front end programming, and even working on the mechanics of programs and apps themselves through languages like Javascript.

I'm currently a student at Diablo Valley College pursuing a degree in Cognitive Science in hopes of going into the UX/UI design field. When I'm not designing or programming, I love to draw, create music, play piano or guitar, sing, sew, and play video games.
